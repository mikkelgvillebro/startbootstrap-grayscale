About Crypto backers

The digital privacy is threatened by both commercial as well as governmental surveillance. All digital information and traffic is collected, this is why it is important to elucidate in this matter. We host a conference to enlighten students about the data collection, about their digital privacy and how they can regain control of the same.

Crypto Conference march 30th. 2016.

This is the first, but hopefully no the last conference here at the IT University. The attention we have gathered is more than we could ever have hoped for!

Irina Shklovski:
Is there any data that is actually private?

As an associated professor at ITU, Irina works on several different projects ranging from considering issues of privacy and identity to investigating the role of technology in disaster. She is interested in how people use such technologies to cope with adverse circumstances, to maintain their social relationships and navigate and interact in urban spaces.

Pernille Tranberg:
Personal Data Harvest

A journalist who now acts as a dataethics consultant. She is co-founder of DataEthics.eu and a board member of the London-based charity organization CitizenTrust. In 2012, she wrote the bestseller "Fake It" about big data and digital self-defense.
